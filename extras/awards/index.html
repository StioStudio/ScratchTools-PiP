The ScratchTools Awards

Starting in January 2024, ScratchTools is hosting an annual awards show, and anyone is welcome to submit a project of their's.

You may submit any project that you have created already, you do not need to make one specifically for this.

Rules

  • The project must have been shared after February 22nd, 2023
  • You may only submit one project
  • The project must be your own
  • There is only one winner, but you may split the prize with whoever you want
  • Must submit by January 28th at 5 PM PST

Prizes

  • A special badge on your profile viewable with the emoji status feature
  • ScratchTools settings page theme named after you that you get to customize
  • Gift card to a store of your choice
  • Recognition on the awards livestream on February 22nd, 2024
  • Bragging rights, of course
